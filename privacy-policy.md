# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how **Twine: Couples Questions Game** ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our iOS application ("App").

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
We do not require you to create an account. The App works fully without registration.

Custom sessions you create are stored **locally on your device only** and are never transmitted to our servers.

### Automatically Collected Information
We do not collect personal data directly. Third-party services used in the App may collect limited technical data as described in Section 4.

---

## 2. Purchases & Subscriptions

Twine offers in-app purchases including:
- Auto-renewable subscriptions (Annual, Monthly)
- One-time lifetime purchase

All purchases are processed by **Apple App Store**. We do not collect or store your payment information.

Apple's Privacy Policy applies to all transactions:
[https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy)

**To cancel a subscription:**
iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Twine → Cancel

We use **RevenueCat** to manage subscription status and entitlements. RevenueCat may collect:
- A random anonymous user identifier
- Purchase and subscription status
- App version and device OS version

RevenueCat does not collect your name, email, or payment information.
RevenueCat Privacy Policy: [https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy)

---

## 3. Push Notifications

If you grant permission, we may send you local push notifications as reminders. These notifications are scheduled locally on your device and do not involve transmitting personal data to external servers.

You can disable notifications at any time:
iOS Settings → Notifications → Twine

## 5. Data Storage

All user-generated content (custom sessions, app preferences) is stored **locally on your device** using iOS standard storage. This data is not transmitted to any server and is permanently deleted when you uninstall the App.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

Certain session categories in the App contain mature content intended for adults (17+). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 17.
