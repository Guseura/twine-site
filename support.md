## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
Go to iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Twine → Cancel Subscription.

**How do I restore my purchase on a new device?**
Open the App → Settings → Restore Purchases.

**The app is not working correctly. What should I do?**
Try closing and reopening the App. If the issue persists, please email us with a description of the problem and your iOS version.

**Can I get a refund?**
Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit [https://support.apple.com/billing](https://support.apple.com/billing) to request a refund.

**Where is my data stored?**
All your data (custom sessions, preferences) is stored locally on your device only. We do not collect or store any personal data on our servers.

We'd love to hear it! Email us at yurahusak06@icloud.com.
