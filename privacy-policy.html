Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Twine: Couples Questions Game ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our iOS application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide
We do not require you to create an account. The App works fully without registration. Custom sessions you create are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted to our servers.

Automatically Collected Information
We do not collect personal data directly. Third-party services used in the App may collect limited technical data as described in Section 4.

2. Purchases & Subscriptions

Twine offers in-app purchases including:

  • Auto-renewable subscriptions (Annual, Monthly)
  • One-time lifetime purchase

All purchases are processed by Apple App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information. Apple's Privacy Policy: apple.com/legal/privacy

To cancel a subscription:
iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Twine → Cancel

We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. RevenueCat may collect a random anonymous user identifier, purchase status, app version and device OS version. RevenueCat does not collect your name, email, or payment information.
RevenueCat Privacy Policy: revenuecat.com/privacy

3. Push Notifications

If you grant permission, we may send local push notifications as reminders. These are scheduled locally on your device and do not transmit personal data to external servers.

Disable at any time: iOS Settings → Notifications → Twine
