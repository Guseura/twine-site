Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription? Go to iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Twine → Cancel Subscription.
How do I restore my purchase on a new device? Open the App → Settings → Restore Purchases.
The app is not working correctly. What should I do? Try closing and reopening the App. If the issue persists, email us with a description of the problem and your iOS version.
Can I get a refund? Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit support.apple.com/billing to submit a request.
Where is my data stored? All your data (custom sessions, preferences) is stored locally on your device only. We do not collect or store personal data on our servers.
